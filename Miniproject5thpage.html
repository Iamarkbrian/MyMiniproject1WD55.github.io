My Gaming Life and History:

Started out at a very young age I managed to be drawn in playing games mainly because of the environment I grew up with, especially that my neighbors and cousins are all playing video games at that time. The very first device that I experienced playing games was on a Gameboy owned by my cousin who is somehow loves playing games too, we spend a lot of time playing together and enjoying our childhood together with our childhood friends. The second device that I experienced to play was on the PSP and Playstation 2 back on our day these are two of the most popular gaming device that me and my friends enjoyed and played a lot before computers came up and proceeds to become one of the best gaming platform in the world, it is the same time when online games gets popular and hyped because of the many interaction possibilites are happening when playing online games and the joy it will bring are unmeasurable for me because being able to play with my cousins and friends that are miles away brings so much joy for me. Gaming easily became a part of my life even on my adult life because I used to play games in my gaming laptop and play station 4 in order to release stress and to enjoy my moment alone in my room but still managed to be responsible to do more important tasks.
